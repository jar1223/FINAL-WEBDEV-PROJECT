About Me

Learn a little bit about me and what makes me, me!

Hello! I'm Jarin Wilson and I'm a 22 year old studying Marketing at BYU. I am passionate for things like music, dance, photography, and videography - with a knack for storytelling through visual media. Born in the beautiful state of Washington in 2002, I got to enjoy the beauty there before moving down to Arizona where my family now resides. Having had different directions I've wanted to go in my life, I have found a great love for digital marketing and creating ad and social content for brands and influencers. I strive to be excellent in everything I do and bring others unto Christ while doing it - one story at a time.
